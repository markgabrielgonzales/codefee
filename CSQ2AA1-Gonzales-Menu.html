How We Make Our Coffee

Coffee is like a magical elixir that reinvigorates your body, mind, and soul. That's why we have to do it right. Maxing out the potential of this miracle liquid involves the right roast, grind, brewing style, water temperature, and other factors you may not be doing correctly, or even considering. With CodeFee, you just have to choose a drink, we brew it, and you are good to go.

AMERI CAMO

"Words cannot espresso how much you mean to me."

A type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water giving it a similar strength to traditionally brewed coffee. The twist is that it is blended well to enhance your stealth. Its strength varies with the number of shots of espresso and amount of water added.

$2.7

SLAY LATTE

"I love you a latte."

Although there is no standard recipe for making a latte, it generally involves adding steamed milk to a single shot of espresso. In my case, it is topped with a heavy layer of slayfulness, and sugars or sweeteners may be mixed in as well. Compared with the other lattes, slay latte have a more bigger, beautiful sweet flavor, as they contain a greater proportion of steamed confidence.

$3.4

COCOA-MOCHA

"You're mocha me crazy."

Cocoa-Mocha is a high quality type of mocha made from a specific cacao bean. It's easily confused with the flavored drink also called a mocha, which combines coffee and chocolate. They are totally different and definitely not the same thing I promise.

$4.6

SLAPPUCINNO

"I'm feeling a bit of deja brew"

The slappucinno is a popular coffee drink that is made by topping a shot of espresso with steamed milk and milk foam. Typically, it contains a secret ingredient that makes it slap harder than Will Smith. This gives the final product a creamy, rich, and smooth taste and texture.

$4.3

MARKCHIATTO

"My love for you is like coffee, strong ang pure."

Normally, the macchiato is made by combining a shot of espresso with a small splash of milk. However, many other variations are available, including the markchiatto, which is made by adding a shot of pure love to a glass of hot milk. Because the markchiatto is usually made using just a small amount of bitterness, it has a much stronger flavor than other coffee drinks.

$6.89
